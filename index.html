OnePush Media

Simple. Beautiful. Done.

What is OnePush Media?

It’s the easiest app in the world for posting media. Take a photo or video, tap once, and publish it across your social media accounts and personal website – no hassle, no distractions.

Who is it for?

Busy creators, travel bloggers, local businesses, or anyone who wants to stay active online without wasting time. Designed especially for mobile-first users who want to publish fast and often.

How does it work?

You open the app, take a photo or video, edit quickly if needed, and press one button. The app handles the rest – posting your content everywhere you’ve connected.

Why was it created?

This project was created by a retired developer who wanted to prove that even from the beach, with no office and no team, you can launch a global app using only an iPhone and iPad.
